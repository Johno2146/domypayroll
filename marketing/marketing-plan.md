# Do My Payroll - Zero-Cost Marketing Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
Do My Payroll provides a high-value, low-friction payroll service for UK SMEs. Our marketing strategy focuses on establishing authority, building local visibility, and leveraging partnerships to acquire clients without significant ad spend.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **UK SMEs:** 2-50 employees.
*   **Sectors:** Hospitality (pubs, restaurants), Retail, Construction, Professional Services (clinics, small agencies).
*   **Persona:** The "Overwhelmed Owner" who handles everything and fears HMRC non-compliance.

## 3. Core Tactics (£0 Budget)

### A. Content Authority
*   **Educational Blog:** Focus on "How-To" and "Compliance Alerts".
*   **LinkedIn Presence:** Weekly insights on UK payroll legislation and business efficiency.

### B. Local SEO & Visibility
*   **Google Business Profile (GBP):** Optimise for local search terms (e.g., "Payroll services Manchester").
*   **Business Directories:** Listing in free UK directories (Yell, Scoot, etc.).

### C. Strategic Partnerships
*   **Accountants & Bookkeepers:** Many accountants dislike payroll admin. We offer a reliable referral partner or "white label" service.
*   **Co-working Spaces:** Providing free "Payroll 101" clinics for members.

### D. Referral Programme
*   Incentivise existing clients to refer other business owners.

### E. Direct Outreach
*   Targeted LinkedIn outreach to business owners in high-need sectors.

## 4. Key Messaging
*   "Run your business, not your payroll."
*   "Compliant, error-free, on time. Every time."
*   "HMRC penalties are optional. Our service makes sure you avoid them."

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   Number of inbound enquiries per month.
*   Organic search traffic to the website.
*   Number of active referral partners.
*   Client acquisition cost (Target: <£10 for free channels).
