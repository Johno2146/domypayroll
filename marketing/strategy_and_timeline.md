# Do My Payroll - Marketing Strategy & Growth Timeline

## 1. Target Audience Deep Dive (UK SMEs)
*   **Hospitality (Pubs, Restaurants, Cafes):** High turnover, complex rotas, tips (Tronc) management, and seasonal staff. Payroll is a weekly burden.
*   **Retail (Independent Shops):** Part-time staff, fluctuating hours, and high compliance risk with Minimum Wage brackets.
*   **Construction (Small Contractors):** CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) requirements are a major pain point. They need CIS-ready payroll.
*   **Professional Services (Clinics, Small Agencies):** Often have high-salaried staff and need precise benefit/pension management.

## 2. Free Marketing Channels (Ranked by ROI)
1.  **Strategic Partnerships (Accountants/Bookkeepers):** Highest ROI. One partner can bring 10+ clients.
2.  **Local SEO (Google Business Profile):** High intent. Owners search for "payroll services near me".
3.  **LinkedIn Authority Building:** High trust. Networking with other business owners and HR consultants.
4.  **Referral Programme:** Leverages existing "social proof" to acquire similar clients.
5.  **Direct Outreach:** High effort, but effective for niche targeting (e.g., CIS contractors).

## 3. Partnership Strategy
*   **The "Accountant Rescue":** Approach firms that don't want the hassle of payroll. Offer a "White Label" service where they keep the client relationship, but we do the work.
*   **Coworking Hubs:** Offer a monthly "Payroll & Pension" free clinic for members.
*   **Local Chambers:** Guest speak on "2024 Compliance Changes" to get in front of dozens of owners at once.

## 4. Referral Programme: "The Partner Reward"
*   **Referrer:** £50 credit per successful sign-up (after 3 months).
*   **New Client:** 50% off onboarding or first month free.

## 5. 30/60/90 Day Growth Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)
*   Optimise Google Business Profile and list on top 5 UK directories.
*   Publish first 4 blog posts and launch the LinkedIn presence.
*   Set up the Referral Programme infrastructure.
*   **Goal:** First organic enquiry.

### Phase 2: Outreach & Partnerships (Days 31-60)
*   Identify and contact 20 local accountants/bookkeepers for partnerships.
*   Start weekly LinkedIn "Compliance Alerts" to build authority.
*   Execute direct outreach to 50 local hospitality/construction businesses.
*   **Goal:** 3-5 new clients via partnerships.

### Phase 3: Scaling & Local Dominance (Days 61-90)
*   Host a free webinar or local clinic on "SME Growth & Payroll Efficiency".
*   Review and scale the best-performing partnership channels.
*   Optimise on-page SEO based on initial traffic data.
*   **Goal:** Reach 10+ active clients and stable MRR.
