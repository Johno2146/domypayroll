# Do My Payroll - Referral Programme Concept

## 1. Goal
To turn our existing happy clients into a secondary sales force by rewarding them for successful introductions.

## 2. The Offer: "The Payroll Partner Reward"
*   **For the Referrer (Existing Client):** £50 credit towards their next bill (effectively one free base-fee month) for every client they refer who stays for at least 3 months.
*   **For the Referee (New Client):** 50% off their onboarding fee (if applicable) or 20% off their first 3 months.

## 3. How It Works
1.  **Unique Code:** Give each client a simple referral code (e.g., "REFRAL-123").
2.  **Simple Submission:** A link on our website where they can "Introduce a Friend" or just mention the name.
3.  **Automatic Credit:** Once the new client has paid their third invoice, the credit is applied to the referrer's account.

## 4. Promotion Tactics
*   **Email Footer:** Add a simple line to every email: "Love our service? Refer a friend and get £50 credit!"
*   **In-App / On-Payslip:** Mention the program occasionally in the client portal.
*   **Direct Ask:** After a successful "win" (e.g., resolving a complex HMRC issue), send a personal note: "We're glad we could help! If you know other business owners who are struggling with this, we'd love to help them too (and reward you in the process)."

## 5. Partner Program (for Accountants/Bookkeepers)
*   For professional partners who refer multiple clients, offer a recurring 10% commission or a significant "white label" discount if they want to resell our services under their own brand.
