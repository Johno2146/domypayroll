# Do My Payroll - Social Media Calendar (1 Month)

## Strategy Overview
*   **LinkedIn (Primary):** B2B networking, authority building, professional partnership development.
*   **Twitter/X:** Real-time news updates (HMRC alerts), joining local business hashtag hours.
*   **Facebook (Local Groups):** Hyper-local targeting in "Business in [City]" groups.

## Weekly Cadence
| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | LinkedIn | Authority / Blog | Weekly Blog Post (e.g., Compliance Guide) |
| **Tue** | X / Twitter | News / Alerts | Quick Compliance Tip or HMRC news |
| **Wed** | Facebook | Local Value | "Free Payroll Health Check for [City] Businesses" |
| **Thu** | LinkedIn | Networking | Tagging a partner or local business event |
| **Fri** | All | Culture / Trust | "Weekend Peace of Mind" post |

## Content Calendar: Week 1 Example

**Monday (LinkedIn):**
*   **Post:** Link to "The 2024 UK SME Payroll Compliance Checklist" blog.
*   **Caption:** Are you 100% sure you're HMRC-ready? We've mapped out the 4 biggest risks for SMEs this year.

**Tuesday (Twitter/X):**
*   **Post:** "Quick Tip: The NLW increase to £11.44 now applies to 21 & 22-year-olds. Double-check your age brackets today! #SMEPayroll #UKBusiness"

**Wednesday (Facebook - Local Groups):**
*   **Post:** "Hi everyone! I'm [Your Name] from Do My Payroll. We're a local firm helping small businesses in [City] get their head around the recent tax changes. If you're doing payroll on Sunday nights, we should talk! Free advice always available."

**Friday (LinkedIn):**
*   **Post:** Photo of a happy team or empty desk.
*   **Caption:** Our clients are logging off for the weekend knowing their payroll is 100% sorted. Compliance is an insurance policy for your sleep. 😴

## Repeat Pattern for Weeks 2-4
*   **Week 2 Focus:** Efficiency & Cost Saving (Outsourcing ROI).
*   **Week 3 Focus:** Sector Spotlight (e.g., "Why pubs have the hardest payroll").
*   **Week 4 Focus:** Referral Program launch and Trust (Testimonials).
