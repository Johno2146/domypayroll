# Do My Payroll - SEO Quick Wins

## 1. Keyword Research (Targeting UK Intent)

### High-Intent "Commercial" Keywords
*   "Small business payroll services UK"
*   "Managed payroll services for SMEs"
*   "Payroll outsourcing Manchester" (and other local cities)
*   "CIS payroll service for construction"
*   "Hospitality payroll services UK"

### Informational "Long-Tail" Keywords (for Blog)
*   "HMRC RTI filing deadlines 2024"
*   "National Living Wage 21 year olds 2024"
*   "How to set up workplace pension UK"
*   "Switching payroll providers checklist"
*   "Is IR35 for employers or employees?"

## 2. On-Page SEO Recommendations
*   **Title Tags:** Use the format `[Service] [Location/Sector] | Do My Payroll`.
    *   Example: `SME Payroll Services Manchester | Do My Payroll`
*   **Meta Descriptions:** Include a call to action and the price point.
    *   Example: `Affordable, compliant payroll for UK SMEs from £50/month. We handle RTI, pensions, and HMRC compliance so you don't have to. Get a free quote today.`
*   **Header Tags (H1, H2):** Ensure the H1 contains "Payroll Services for [City/Sector]".
*   **Image Alt Text:** Label graphics as "UK SME Payslip Example" or "HMRC Compliance Guide".

## 3. Local SEO Checklist (GBP)
1.  **Google Business Profile:** Claim, verify, and complete 100% of the profile.
2.  **Category Selection:** Primary: "Payroll Service". Secondary: "Accounting Firm", "Business Consultant".
3.  **Local Landing Pages:** Create a page for `/payroll-services-manchester` or `/payroll-services-london`.
4.  **NAP Consistency:** Ensure Name, Address, and Phone are identical on the website and all directories (Yell, Scoot, etc.).
5.  **Review Strategy:** Request a review from every client after their first successful pay run with us.

## 4. Technical SEO Basics
*   **Speed:** Ensure the site loads in under 2 seconds (SME owners are mobile and busy).
*   **Mobile-First:** Ensure the "Contact" button is easy to tap on a phone.
*   **XML Sitemap:** Submit to Google Search Console as soon as the site is live.
