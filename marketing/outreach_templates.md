# Do My Payroll - Outreach Templates & Follow-up Sequences

## 1. Partnership Outreach (to Accountants/Bookkeepers)
**Subject:** Freeing up your firm's time / [Accountancy Firm Name]

**Initial Message (LinkedIn or Email):**
Hi [Name],

I’m [Your Name] from Do My Payroll. We specialize in managed payroll for SMEs in the [Region/Sector].

I know many accounting firms find payroll admin to be a low-margin distraction from their core advisory work. We work with several firms to handle their clients' payroll as a dedicated partner—either through direct referrals or on a white-label basis.

Are you open to a brief call to see if a partnership would add value to your firm and your clients?

**Follow-up 1 (Day 3):**
Hi [Name], just checking if you saw my note. Even if you're happy with your current setup, it might be worth knowing us as a "Plan B" for when your team is at capacity during year-end.

**Follow-up 2 (Day 10):**
Hi [Name], I'll stop bugging you after this! I just wanted to share our "Accountant Referral Guide" which outlines how we provide a kickback/credit to firms that partner with us. I'll leave it here if you ever need a reliable pair of hands.
