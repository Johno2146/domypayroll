# Do My Payroll - Content Strategy

## 1. Objectives
*   Build trust with UK SME owners.
*   Establish authority on UK payroll compliance.
*   Drive organic traffic via high-intent keywords.

## 2. Key Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Compliance & Legislation
*   **Target:** Business owners worried about HMRC.
*   **Topics:** Minimum Wage updates, National Insurance changes, RTI requirements, Year-end payroll processing.
*   **Format:** "The Essential Guide to...", "Compliance Checklists".

### Pillar 2: Business Efficiency
*   **Target:** Busy founders and office managers.
*   **Topics:** Time-saving tips for admin, Why outsourcing saves money, Scaling your team without admin headaches.
*   **Format:** Case studies, ROI calculators (text-based), Comparison guides.

### Pillar 3: Employee Wellbeing
*   **Target:** Progressive SMEs.
*   **Topics:** Financial wellbeing for employees, Why accurate pay matters for retention, Managing benefits.
*   **Format:** Tips for employers, "Best practice" articles.

## 3. Platform Strategy

### Website Blog
*   **Frequency:** 1 high-quality post per week.
*   **SEO Focus:** Long-tail keywords (e.g., "Small business payroll help London").

### LinkedIn (Personal & Company)
*   **Frequency:** 2-3 updates per week.
*   **Style:** Opinionated, helpful, and personable.
*   **Focus:** Networking with other business owners and potential partners (accountants).

## 4. Proposed Blog Topics (First 4 Weeks)
1.  **Week 1:** "5 Common Payroll Mistakes That Cost UK SMEs Thousands in HMRC Fines"
2.  **Week 2:** "The 2024 Minimum Wage Increase: A Survival Guide for Hospitality Owners"
3.  **Week 3:** "In-house vs Outsourced Payroll: Which is Right for Your Growing Business?"
4.  **Week 4:** "Pensions Auto-Enrolment: Your Ongoing Duties Explained Simply"

## 5. Content Repurposing
*   One blog post -> 3 LinkedIn posts -> 1 Newsletter snippet -> 1 PDF Checklist for lead magnet.
